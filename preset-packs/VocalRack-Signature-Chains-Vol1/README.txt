VocalRack — Signature Chains Vol. 1
====================================

8 ready-to-mix vocal chain presets for VocalRack (VocalEssential Suite).

INSTALL
-------
Option A (in the plugin): open VocalRack, click the preset dropdown,
choose "Import Preset...", and pick each .vepreset file.

Option B (copy the files):
  macOS:   ~/Library/Application Support/VocalEssential/VocalRack/Presets/
  Windows: C:\Users\<you>\AppData\Roaming\VocalEssential\VocalRack\Presets\
Restart the plugin (or reopen the dropdown) and the presets appear.

THE PRESETS
-----------
  Drake Mix Chain    — the flagship OVO-style voicing: tight gate, smooth comp,
                       controlled air, short slap + plate glue.
  Clean Pop Chain    — transparent polish for pop leads, extra air, light clip.
  Aggressive Rap     — hard gate, heavy comp, HEAT drive and a loud clip stage.
  Warm RnB           — dark heat, mud carved out, big plate, silky top.
  Airy Ballad        — wide-open air band, long plate, slow delay throws.
  Podcast Dry Voice  — sends off, broadcast-ready dry chain.
  Hyperpop Blowout   — maxed heat + clip, bright and in-your-face.
  Late Night Melodic — saturated lo-heat, deep delay/verb wash for melodic cuts.

Requires VocalRack 1.0.0 or later (sold separately / included in the
VocalEssential Suite). Presets are plain parameter files — no license needed.
